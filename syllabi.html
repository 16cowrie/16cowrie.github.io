Module 0 — Course Overview

This training introduces Iyawo, priests, and priestesses to the sacred art of Merindilogun “20-4” (Sixteen Cowrie Shell Divination). The modules build discipline, competence, confidence, and spiritual clarity through structure and daily practice.

Module 1 — Daily Casting Foundations

  • Cast 16 times daily for 16 consecutive days.
  • Log date, count, seniority, Orisha, 3-word summary.
  • Develop consistency + energy attunement.

Module 2 — Seniority Determination

  • Cast twice to compare seniority.
  • Senior = (+); Junior = (–).
  • Foundation for Ire vs. Ibi discernment.

Module 3 — Distinguishing Ire vs Ibi

  • Create Elder and Junior Odu pairs.
  • Elder senior ? Ire.
  • Junior dominant ? Ibi.

Module 4 — Dot Marking System

  • Mark first pair as Ire/Ibi based on second pair.
  • Use dot notation: single or double dots.

Module 5 — Sources of Ire/Ibi (Igbo Method)

  • Final cast: Igbo-Ire on up-cowries, Igbo-Ibi on down-cowries.
  • If Ibi: check Iku (death), Ofo (loss), Ija (conflict), Oran (legal trouble).
  • If Ire: check Aiku (longevity), Aje (prosperity), Rin (relationships), Omo (children).

Module 6 — Course Flow

  • Zoom instruction + workbook + audio files.
  • 8–10 week foundation track.
  • Weekly log submissions for review.
  • Certificate upon practicum completion.

Module 7 — Policies

  • Open to Iyawo and sincere seekers.
  • Maintain confidentiality and cultural respect.
  • Divination is sacred work — uphold SGI ethics.
